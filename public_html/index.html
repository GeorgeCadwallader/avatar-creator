Avatr

Welcome to Avatr!


Use this app to create your own custom avatar that you can use for any site! Apply different shapes from the four layers, change their site, rotation, colour and layer hierarchy. Adapt all this to your advantage to create whatever you can imagine! After you have created something you are happy with click the download button and you will download your creation as a JPG.
